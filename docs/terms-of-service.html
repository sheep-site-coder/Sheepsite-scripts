1. Description of Services

SheepSite LLC provides a hosted platform designed to help Florida condominium associations meet the website requirements of Florida Statute §718.111(12). Services include:

  • Public document storage — a browsable, publicly accessible archive of association documents
  • Private document storage — resident-only access to non-public documents, protected by individual login credentials
  • Resident account management — tools to create, update, and remove resident login accounts
  • Reports — elevator, parking, board of directors, and resident lists generated from association data
  • Woolsy — an AI-assisted FAQ chatbot trained on general Florida condominium statutes and association-specific governing documents (see Section 7)

2. Data We Collect and Store

SheepSite LLC collects only the minimum information needed to operate the service. This includes:

  • Resident names, unit numbers, and optional contact information (email address, phone numbers) as entered by the Administrator
  • Usernames and bcrypt-hashed passwords — passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Vehicle registration information (make, color, tag) as optionally provided
  • Documents and files uploaded by the Administrator to the association's Drive folders, including association financial documents required to be maintained under Florida Statute §718.111(12) (such as budgets, financial statements, audits, and meeting minutes)
  • Usage logs (login counts, chatbot question counts) used for service analytics — not shared with third parties

Association financial documents vs. personal financial information: Florida law requires associations to maintain certain financial records accessible to unit owners. SheepSite LLC's private storage is designed to host these association-level documents. However, SheepSite LLC does not collect, store, or process any individual resident's personal financial information — including bank account numbers, credit card numbers, payment history, liens, assessments owed, or any other personal financial data. Associations should never upload personal financial information of individual residents to this platform.

We do not collect individual financial account or payment information, Social Security numbers, government-issued identification, medical records, or any other sensitive personal data as defined under Florida or federal law.
