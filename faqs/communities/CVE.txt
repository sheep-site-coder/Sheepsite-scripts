## THE THREE GOVERNING ENTITIES AT CVE

Century Village East is governed by three separate non-profit corporations. Each has
its own rules, fees, and authority. They do not speak for each other.

1. YOUR BUILDING ASSOCIATION (e.g., Lyndhurst H Condominium Association)
   - Governs: your specific building — unit rules, assessments, parking, pets,
     alterations, guest occupancy in units, etc.
   - Contact: your building's Board of Directors
   - Rules vary building to building

2. CVE MASTER MANAGEMENT CO., INC.
   - Governs: community-wide infrastructure — security/gate access, roads,
     irrigation, drainage/lakes, buses, utilities liaison, contractor access
   - Contact: 954-421-5566 | customerservice@cvedb.com
     3501 West Drive, Deerfield Beach, FL 33442
     Hours: 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM weekdays (2:00 PM Fridays in summer)
   - All 8,508 unit owners sign a CVE Master Management Agreement upon purchase

3. CENCLUB RECREATION MANAGEMENT, INC.
   - Governs: all recreational facilities — Clubhouse, pools, fitness center,
     sports courts, entertainment, classes, dining
   - Contact: 954-428-6892 | admin@cenclub.com | cenclub.com
     2400 Century Blvd., Deerfield Beach, FL 33442
   - Monthly fee (2025): $110.00/month per unit (mandatory, paid to CenClub)

COOCVE (Condominium Owners Organization of CVE) is a fourth body that provides
education and support for building associations — it has no governing or enforcement
authority over residents directly.

## CVE MASTER MANAGEMENT — COMMUNITY RULES

### Water Use
- Use of city water by individual residents for ANY outdoor purpose is PROHIBITED.
  This includes lawn watering, garden watering, and car washing.
- CVE operates its own dedicated $11 million irrigation system (upgraded 2016).
  Residents are not permitted to supplement with city water.
- The only exception: associations may use city water for routine power washing
  with written permission from CVE Master Management.

### Car Washing
- Washing your car with hose water is prohibited in the village.
- Mobile vehicle washing / detailing services are NOT permitted at CVE
  (City of Deerfield Beach rule enforced at CVE).

### Irrigation / Watering Schedule (SFWMD / Broward County)
These restrictions apply to CVE's irrigation system and all outdoor water use
in Broward County under South Florida Water Management District rules:
- Watering PROHIBITED daily between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM
- Permitted only between 5:00 PM and 9:00 AM
- PROHIBITED on Mondays, Tuesdays, and Fridays
- Odd-numbered addresses: permitted Wednesdays and Saturdays only
- Even-numbered addresses: permitted Thursdays and Sundays only
- Hand watering with a self-closing nozzle: permitted at any time (SFWMD exemption)
- Broward County is NOT currently under any additional water shortage order (as of early 2026)

## CENCLUB RECREATION MANAGEMENT — FACILITY RULES

### What CenClub Operates
- 145,000 sq. ft. Clubhouse (open 363 days/year; closed Thanksgiving and Christmas Day)
- Outdoor Clubhouse Pool and Indoor Clubhouse Pool (with locker rooms and saunas)
- 14 satellite pools distributed throughout the village (including Lyndhurst North and South)
- Fitness Center
- Tennis courts, pickleball courts, handball/racquetball, shuffleboard, bocce, volleyball
- Ballroom, movie theater, dining/restaurant, library, card rooms, hobby/arts rooms

### Hours of Operation
- Clubhouse general: open until 11:00 PM daily (equipment must be returned by 11 PM)
- Staff Office: Monday–Sunday 8:00 AM – 11:00 PM
- Outdoor Clubhouse Pool: 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM daily
- Indoor Clubhouse Pool: 7:00 AM – 9:45 PM daily
- Satellite Pools: gates open 7:00 AM, locked at 10:00 PM
- Administration Office: Monday–Friday 9:00 AM – 4:30 PM
- ID/Payment Office: Mon–Fri 9:00 AM – 4:30 PM; Wednesdays until 6:30 PM (Dec–Mar)
- Athletic Office: Monday–Friday 6:00 AM – 2:00 PM
- Class Office: Monday–Friday 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM
- Library: Monday–Thursday 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM

### ID Card Requirement
- All persons using CenClub recreation facilities may be required to show their CVE-issued ID card.
- Anyone without their ID card must immediately remove themselves from the facilities.
- Any ID card found in the possession of anyone other than the person it was issued to will be confiscated.

### General Conduct Rules
- All persons must follow instructions and decisions of the Executive Director and staff.
- All persons must conduct themselves in a courteous manner with due regard for others' rights.
- No wagering, gambling, lotteries, or raffles except where authorized by law.
- No solicitations without written consent of management.
- Residents are responsible for their guests and must inform guests of the rules.
- Facilities shall not be used by any group, club, or association without written consent of management.
- Vehicles may be parked only in designated areas. Illegally parked vehicles will be towed.
- Overnight parking is prohibited in the Clubhouse lot.

### Pets / Animals at CenClub Facilities
- No dogs or other animals are allowed at any CenClub facility.
- This includes emotional support animals, comfort animals, and therapy animals.
- Service animals are the only exception, governed by Florida Statutes §413.08.
- NOTE: This is a CenClub facility rule — pet rules inside your unit are set by
  your building association and are separate.

### Children / Minors at CenClub
- No person under 18 is admitted to the Clubhouse, Clubhouse Pools, or most
  recreation facilities, except in designated areas assigned by management.
- Children ARE permitted at: ping-pong area, outdoor courts, BBQ areas,
  putt-putt course, and satellite pools (with adult supervision).
- Children under 14 must be accompanied by an adult (18+) with a Membership Pass
  or Nanny Pass at all times, including at pools.

### Dress Code
- Shirts and shoes must be worn at all times inside the Clubhouse.
  Exception: pool deck and locker rooms.
- During season (November 1 – March 31): no shorts at Saturday Night Dances.
- Off-season (April 1 – October 31): shorts generally permitted in the Clubhouse.

### Guest Rules at CenClub
- Residents may bring up to TWO guests at a time.
- Guests may use most facilities when accompanied by a resident, EXCEPT instructional
  and fitness/athletic classes (residents only for classes).
- Fitness Center: guests permitted after 1:00 PM only (not before).
- Pools (Clubhouse outdoor, indoor, and satellite): guests welcome when accompanied
  by a resident.
- Extended stays / unaccompanied guests: a guest pass (up to 30 days) may be obtained
  through the Staff Office or ID Office. With a guest pass, guests may access facilities
  without the resident being present.
- Visitors without a guest pass must be accompanied by a resident and show a valid
  driver's license to enter the Clubhouse.

### Fitness Center Rules
- Fitness/athletic classes: CVE residents and renters only — no guests in classes.
- A signed waiver is required to use the Fitness Center.
- 30-minute time limit on cardio equipment (treadmills, ellipticals, bikes).
- Rubber-soled shoes required. No wedges, open-toe shoes, flip-flops, or golf shoes.
- No food or drink — bottled water only.
- Do not wear perfume or cologne.
- Always wipe down equipment after use with provided solution.
- No one under age 18 allowed.

### Pool Rules (Saunas — Indoor Pool)
- Saunas: 15-minute maximum use; dry heat only; clothing/towel required.
- "Bring a buddy" policy applies in saunas.
- Do not add water to sauna heating elements.
